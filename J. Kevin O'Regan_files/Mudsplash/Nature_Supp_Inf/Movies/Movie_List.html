(NB. The timing in these web demonstrations does not accurately reflect the timing used in the experiments we performed. In the real experiments, the duration of the mudplashes or flicker  was generally 80 ms,  much shorter than what will usually occur on a web demonstration. However this is not critical for the effect to occur, so long as the mudsplashes or flicker  appear all at once. If they are appearing progressively, then please try a faster computer or faster internet connection).
